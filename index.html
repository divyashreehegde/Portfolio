Self Driving Car

- Trained a CNN to map raw pixels from 3 front facing cameras directly to steering commands. Predicted steering angle based on the images of road is fed to the steering and hence can drive on its own.
- This is an end-to-end approach which includes data collection from a simulator called Udacity (Nanodegree program), data processing, augmentation of images, model building and testing the model on the simulator.
- This is an implementation of NVIDIA paper and model created by NVIDIA which eliminates the entire task of lane detection and path planning or control.
- Advantage of this project is that a small amount of training data is sufficient to train the car to operate in diverse conditions, on highways, local and residential roads.
